Who drew all of the awesome pictures?

Great question! None other than kaiju afficionado, Lisa Naffziger. I've had the privilege of knowing Lisa for a little over three years now, not only as a fellow giant monster fan but also, and more importantly, as someone I would consider a dear friend. (She has also contributed some great artwork for a few of the podcasts I've created!) She has several graphic novels that are out now, along with a few that have been picked up for publication, so make sure to check out her portfolio. Also, she has a Society6 page with some wonderful designs! (And yes, I did get permission to use them on here!)
